his page provides an up-to-date overview of Internet Computer standards that are nearing completion, actively being developed, or have already been approved by the relevant working groups or the broader community.
Its purpose is to offer a clear snapshot of the standards ecosystem as it evolves—highlighting the most relevant, mature, and impactful proposals without listing early drafts or inactive ideas.

The standards included here represent work that is either:

In the final stages of design or review

Actively progressing with consensus and engagement

Formally adopted or endorsed through a working group or community approval process

This page will be updated periodically as standards advance through the lifecycle from proposal to adoption.
